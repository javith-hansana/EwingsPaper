01 About Me

Javith Hansana is an experienced audio engineer and sound designer based in Colombo, Sri Lanka. With a deep passion for sound and technical precision, Javith specializes in music production, sound design, mixing, mastering, and surround sound engineering.

Having worked with leading artists, filmmakers, and event organizers, Javith is known for his attention to detail and dedication to delivering high-quality audio experiences. At JH Music Studios, he brings creative visions to life, ensuring that each project resonates with clarity and impact.

Whether it's crafting immersive soundscapes for films, producing music, or providing expert audio mixing and mastering, Javith's expertise ensures a professional and polished result every time.
